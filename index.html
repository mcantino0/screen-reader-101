Screen Readers 101

JAWS and Basic Computer Navigation

In this course, we'll learn all about screen readers. Screen readers are an important piece of technology for blind users, and they can be found on nearly every modern computer, tablet, or smartphone. In this course, we'll focus on screen readers on computers. The course begins with a review of commonly used keys and their functions. From there, we'll explore how to use this small set of keys to complete a wide variety of tasks on the computer, including basic navigation, working with applications, organizing files, and editing text.
